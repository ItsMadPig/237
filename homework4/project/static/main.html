  • Instructions
    Click on icons to choose different Bullets
    Normal Bullets: -1 Health
    Fire Bullets: -2 Health
    Lightning Bullets: -2 Health with super cool lightning

    Press Left and Right key to move.
    Press Up and Down to adjust angle.

    Hold Space bar to Charge
    Release Space bar to Shoot
